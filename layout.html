Different Types of Curly Hair

The different types of curls can be broken down into three main categories: wavy, curly, and coily. While there are three main curly hair types, there are many variations of each, and no one is confined to just one variation. ‌ Based on a few defining features that we’ve outlined below, you will be able to figure out your own curl type. While it may be obvious right away, keep in mind that you might have a combination of these types, which is important to note when it comes to care and styling (more on that later).
